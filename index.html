me

While considering that polluting the web with mindless things is not a good practice, I need to exhibit my digital skills for some academic purpose.

I'm a cross border, born in Eastern Europe, having French nationality, living in Ireland, speaking five languages (and understanding three or four more.)

I hold an MA in Art History, have a long experience as an Art Teacher and a short one, as Web Designer. Not sure what for, since recently, I spend a lot of time doing creative coding. On your left (or top, if your screen is a mobile one), one of my painstacking exercises in P5.js.

I'm interested in Cultural Analytics' approach, in interactive Data Visualisation and Generative Art. I try to learn about all of that, via books and Internet. Hoping to find a support from educational institution I started, in September 2018, an MA in Digital Arts and Humanities on University College of Cork.

This site is created to record my learning progress. It is hand made on Brackets, free of Wordpress, slow-cooked with HTML5, SVG, CSS Grid and Variables ingredients, with some Javascript.
